SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY — KC JEWELLERS

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Delivery Availability
Delivery availability depends entirely on our logistics partners and serviceability of the delivery location.
We do not guarantee delivery to all locations.

2. Shipping Charges
Shipping is not free.
Delivery charges are fully payable by the customer.
Charges vary depending on:
Delivery partner
Package weight
Delivery location

3. Dispatch Timeline
Orders are generally dispatched within 4–5 business days.
While delays are unlikely, in case of unforeseen issues or product unavailability, the order may be cancelled and refunded accordingly.

4. Delivery Process
Orders will be delivered through third-party logistics partners.
Delivery timelines depend on partner availability and location.

5. Risk & Responsibility
Once dispatched, the responsibility of delivery lies with the logistics partner.
Customers must ensure accurate address and availability to receive the shipment.
