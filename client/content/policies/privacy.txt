PRIVACY POLICY — KC JEWELLERS

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Data We Collect
We collect necessary personal information including:

Name
Mobile number (for OTP authentication)
Email address
Shipping address
Order and SIP history

2. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used strictly for:

Processing and fulfilling orders
Managing SIP accounts and rate bookings
Sending transactional updates (SMS/WhatsApp)
Compliance with Indian financial and tax regulations

3. Payment Processing
We do not store sensitive payment details. Payments are processed through secure and compliant third-party payment partners.

4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. Information is shared only with:

Delivery/logistics partners
Payment processing providers

5. Your Rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal requirements.
