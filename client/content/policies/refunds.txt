REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY — KC JEWELLERS

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Standard E-commerce Orders

No Cancellation: Once an order is placed, it cannot be cancelled by the customer under any circumstances.
No Return / No Exchange: All products sold are final. We do not accept returns, exchanges, or replacements after delivery. Customers are advised to carefully review product details before placing an order.

2. Systematic Investment Plans (SIP)

Strict No-Cash-Refund Policy: Contributions made toward Gold, Silver, or Diamond SIPs are strictly non-refundable in cash.
Redemptions: Upon maturity or premature closure of an SIP, the accumulated value can only be redeemed against physical jewellery available in our catalog or store.

3. Rate Bookings

Advances paid to lock live market rates are non-refundable. If the customer does not proceed with the final purchase, the advance amount will be converted into store credit.

4. Refund Processing

In cases where a refund is approved solely at the discretion of KC Jewellers, the amount may be processed through any suitable payment method, including bank transfer, digital payment, store adjustment, or other mutually agreed methods, within a reasonable processing period.
